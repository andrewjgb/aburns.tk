Experience

social media | SEO | marketing

After graduating from the University of Edinburgh with a degree in Ecological Sciences, I worked for Europe’s largest conservation charity, theRSPB, within their Public Affairs and Marketing department. Following my time at the RSPB and travelling for six months, I then began working for a small sustainability consultancywhere I was responsible for developing the company’s digital marketing and communications strategy. Currently, I am based in Bristol where I provide social media management, search engine optimisation and marketing services to small businesses. My interest in social media, SEO and marketing has evolved naturally over my career. Search engine optimisation and the content creation and social media management tied to this subject is an area I am particularly interested in. Copywriting and editing is another skill which I have a natural flair for and I enjoy utilising. I am keen to become involved in projects which would utilise my SEO, social media management, and copywriting, marketing and communications expertise. Take a look at the services I offer and contact me if you would like to utilise my expertise.

During my time studying Ecological Sciences, I developed a passion for the environment, sustainability issues and permaculture. I explored this subject in more detail while travelling and volunteering at WWOOF farms in the UK and Spain. During this time, I volunteered at a beautiful yoga retreat, Suryalila, where I learnt about organic farming and practised yoga daily. During my time managing the marketing and communications for a small sustainability consultancy, I developed my copywriting and editing skills and I have become an experienced writer covering sustainability issues. My areas of interest are not limited to sustainability, I love fashion and I get to share this with my friends in Bristol who are starting out as textile, accessory and clothing designers. I love to write in the areas of fashion, nature, sustainability, yoga and I collect imagery on all these subjects as I develop my photography skills. Read more about these subject areas and anything else that interests me on my blog.
